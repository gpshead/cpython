Add an *on_error* keyword-only argument to the
:class:`concurrent.futures.Executor` subclass constructors.  It controls what
happens to outstanding futures when the executor is used as a context manager
and the :keyword:`with` block raises an exception: ``"wait"`` (the default,
today's behavior), ``"cancel"`` and, for
:class:`~concurrent.futures.ProcessPoolExecutor`, ``"terminate"`` and
``"kill"``.  A callable returning one of those actions may be supplied to
decide based on the exception.  Patch by Gregory P. Smith.
